Is conversion the right path for me right now?

Common signs you’re ready to start:

  • You feel a sustained connection to Judaism (not just a recent interest).
  • You’re open to consistent learning and community participation.
  • Your motivation goes beyond marriage/administrative needs.
  • You want clarity on movements (Orthodox, Conservative, Reform) and requirements.
  • You’re willing to ask questions and receive guidance.
  • You can dedicate 2–5 hours a week to study and practice.

FAQs

Short answers to common questions

Is this meeting suitable for me?
It’s ideal if you're unsure whether conversion is right for you—or if you've committed but don’t know how to begin. It’s not intended for those already in the final stages of conversion or preparing for a rabbinical court.
What does the meeting include?
  • A step‑by‑step walkthrough of the conversion process: how to start, what comes next, and what to expect at each stage.
  • Explanation of different types of conversion in Judaism and how they vary.
  • Guidance on budgeting and what to consider financially.
  • Information about conversions recognized by the State of Israel and their implications.
  • Time for your own questions.
How long is the meeting?
45 minutes.
How much does it cost?
USD 35.
What will I receive at the end?
  • Help connecting with a rabbi or conversion program.
  • Ongoing support via WhatsApp/email for questions that arise during your journey.
  • A 14% discount on all books in the store.
What is the rescheduling/cancellation policy?
  • Cancel without penalty if done at least 48 hours in advance.
  • Cancellations made within 48 hours are fully charged.
  • You can reschedule up to 48 hours before the appointment.
  • If we must cancel less than 48 hours before, you’ll receive a full refund.
Additional notes
  • The presentation is not sent afterward due to copyright.
  • Appointments cannot be scheduled more than one month in advance.
  • If you have questions before booking, email customerservice@newchoosenewjews.com.
