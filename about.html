About us

Meet Noa — our founder

A personal story that became a global community: guidance, support, and clarity for those seeking to join the Jewish people.

Home · About

Shalom! I’m Noa, the founder of New Choose New Jews.

I was born in Tel Aviv to a Jewish-Israeli father and a French mother from Alsace. My mother, raised in a Catholic family, always felt drawn to Judaism, inspired by the stories of the Holocaust, and later chose to convert Reform. I was considered irreligious because of my mom’s reform conversion, and regardless was completely disconnected from Jewish life and unaware of the roots that were quietly waiting for me.


Everything began to shift during my military service in the IDF. Initially, I sought the simplest path, a role that felt small and routine — but something inside me longed for more. Serving as an officer, I was exposed to Jewish values, history, and community for the first time in a meaningful way. Observing my colleagues, attending Shabbat meals with a welcoming host family, and participating in Jewish learning programs thanks to the officer track opened my heart. I began to feel, perhaps for the first time, that I belonged somewhere — a world I had never known but had always carried inside me.


After completing my conversion, I realized how challenging this journey can be for those navigating it alone, especially outside of Israel. Many face bureaucratic hurdles, financial barriers, and social challenges in their communities, all while striving to live a Jewish life in non-Jewish environments. I wanted to change that.


I began sharing my story and guidance on social media, helping others understand the process and feel supported. From there, a global community emerged — converts, rabbis, and mentors from around the world, all connecting, learning, and supporting each other.


My mission is to help those seeking to join the Jewish people feel seen, guided, and embraced — so they can connect to their heritage, their faith, and ultimately, themselves.
