FAQs

Short answers to common questions

Is this meeting suitable for me?
It’s ideal if you're unsure whether conversion is right for you—or if you've committed but don’t know how to begin. It’s not intended for those already in the final stages of conversion or preparing for a rabbinical court.
What does the meeting include?
  • A step‑by‑step walkthrough of the conversion process: how to start, what comes next, and what to expect at each stage.
  • Explanation of different types of conversion in Judaism and how they vary.
  • Guidance on budgeting and what to consider financially.
  • Information about conversions recognized by the State of Israel and their implications.
  • Time for your own questions.
