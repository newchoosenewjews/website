Considering conversion to Judaism, with clarity and support

We are a global online community of individuals converting to Judaism, founded by a fellow convert. Our mission is to provide guidance, education, and heartfelt support to anyone seeking to join the Jewish people — no matter where they are in the world.

Whether you’re just beginning to explore your Jewish identity or already immersed in the conversion process, you’ll find a welcoming home here among others walking the same path.
